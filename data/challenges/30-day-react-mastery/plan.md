## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency over intensity** - 1 hour every day beats 7 hours once a week
2. **Code along** - Don't just watch, type the code yourself
3. **Build projects** - Apply what you learn immediately
4. **Ask questions** - Use the chat to clarify concepts
5. **Review regularly** - End-of-week reviews reinforce learning
